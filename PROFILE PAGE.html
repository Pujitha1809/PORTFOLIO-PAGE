Scrapcycle : The Marketplace for Reusable Household Items

ScrapCycle mainly focuses on recycling and repurposing scrap materials. It mainly aims to create an online platform dedicated to the reuse and recycling of household scraps. This project addresses the growing need for sustainable waste management by providing a user-friendly website where individuals can buy and sell second-hand items, including clothes, furniture, electronics, and many more. By facilitating the exchange of reusable goods, ScrapCycle promotes environmental sustainability, reduces landfill waste, and encourages a circular economy. The platform includes features such as detailed item listings, user reviews, secure transactions, and local pickup options to enhance user experience and trust. Ultimately, ScrapCycle seeks to build a community of environmentally conscious consumers who contribute to a greener future through the responsible reuse of household items.
